How to Play: A Step-by-Step Guide

Gameplay Overview

Basketball Stars begins with players choosing their dream team or dream basketball players. Whether you are a long-time fan of the NBA or just a casual gamer looking to have a great time, you only need a few minutes to know about the rules of this game. Here are the key points of Basketball Stars:

  • Each match lasts for one minute only. Players can track the remaining time by looking at the timer in the upper-center corner of the screen. At the end of the match, the team that scores the most points wins the game.
  • Depending on whether players are keeping the ball or not, there are two different strategies: attacking and defending. To achieve victory, players will need to decide which strategy to follow. Prevent your opponents from scoring by jumping to block their shots, or take the initiative, snatch the ball from their hand, and score points!
  • Each player has one of four special abilities. Utilize his ability and cooperate with your teammate to score the most points possible!

That sums up everything you need to know. Now it’s time to start playing on the court!

Basketball Stars Game Controls

1 Player Mode:

  • Move the character with the WASD or arrow keys. Press the keys twice to dash.
  • Shoot or steal the ball with the X or L keys.
  • Pump or block the opponent’s ball with the S or down arrow keys.
  • Execute special abilities with the K or Z keys.

2 Player Mode:

  • Move the character with the WASD (P1) and arrow keys (P2). Press the keys twice to dash.
  • Shoot or steal the ball with the B (P1) or L (P2) keys.
  • Pump or block the opponent’s ball with the S (P1) or down arrow keys (P2).
  • Execute special abilities with the V (P1) or K (P2) keys.

Game Modes

Single Player

In this mode, players can participate in 1v1 or 2v2 matches with the CPU as the opponent. However, regardless of which option you choose, you can only control one player, and your teammate will be directed by the CPU.

  • Tournament: Face off against other teams in three different rounds: quarterfinals, semifinals, and finals, at two levels of difficulty: normal and hard. Be warned that you will have to start from the beginning if you lose a match!
  • Random Match: Pick the members of your and your opponent’s team, and let the game begin!
  • Training: In case you want to become familiar with the extensive controls of Basketball Stars, pick this sub-mode and start dribbling the ball undisturbed by opponents!

Two Players

Let others join the fun with the local 2-player mode! Players can pick between 1v1 and 2v2 matches, but just like in the 1 Player mode, each player can only control one character.

  • 1v1 Matches: Take part in multiple showdowns between two players!
  • 2v2 Matches: In this sub-mode, players can choose to be on the same team and play against the CPU. Or, it is possible for each to have the CPU as their teammate and compete against one another instead.

Quick Match

Choose this mode to play a quick 1v1 match against the CPU with random players.

Special Abilities

  • Mega Dunk: Regardless of where you are on the court, as long as you are holding the ball, you can dunk it through the opponent’s hoop!
  • Iron Defense: Block the opponent’s chance of scoring immediately by turning your basketball hoop into a literal chunk of iron for a few seconds!
  • Alley-Oop: Throw the ball you are holding to the opponent’s side of the court, teleport there, and dunk the ball!
  • Fast Break: Snatch the ball that the opposition is holding and pass through him quickly!

Tips and Tricks

  • While players can try to shoot the hoops as soon as they get hold of the ball, it is best that they get close enough to the hoop to get the highest chance of scoring.
  • For the special abilities Mega Dunk and Alley-Oop, as soon as they are available to players, use them immediately for a 100% chance of scoring.
  • Try to dribble the ball while advancing past opponents to prevent them from grabbing it.
  • Players can score more points per shot when the timer is about to run out. As such, one popular strategy is to concentrate solely on defending the basketball hoop, then be on the offensive towards the end of the match.
  • If you choose 2v2 matches, try to experiment with different special ability combinations and pick the ones that suit your playstyle the best.
